title: "Get started with Quorent"
description: "Sign up with Google, pick up to 10 interest topics, set your delivery time, and get your first personalized AI news digest in under five minutes."
---

Quorent is ready to use the moment you sign in — there's nothing to install and no configuration file to edit. The steps below walk you through signing up, completing onboarding, and getting your first AI digest delivered to your inbox.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up with Google">
    Go to [quorent-rho.vercel.app](https://quorent-rho.vercel.app) and click **Sign in with Google**. Quorent uses Google OAuth via Supabase, so your Google account is the only sign-in method available — no separate password to create.

    After you authenticate, Quorent checks whether you have an existing account. First-time users are automatically redirected to the onboarding page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    On the onboarding page you do two things:

    **Choose your topics.** Select up to 10 topics from the preset list — options include Artificial Intelligence, Finance & Markets, World Politics, Climate & Environment, Technology, Health & Medicine, Space & Astronomy, Startups, and more. You can also type in a custom topic (up to 40 characters) and press **Add** or hit Enter.

    **Set your digest time.** Use the time picker to choose when you want your daily briefing to arrive, then select your timezone from the dropdown. Quorent automatically detects your local timezone and pre-selects it.

    When you're happy with your selections, click **Start My Digest**. Quorent saves your preferences and sends you to the dashboard.

    <Tip>
      Specific topics produce more focused digests than broad ones. "Quantum computing" will surface more targeted stories than just "Technology" — consider mixing a few broad categories with one or two niche interests.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    The dashboard is your home base. From here you can:

    - Browse today's article feed, organized around your selected topics
    - Check the status of your next scheduled digest
    - Review and update your interests at any time

    Your article feed is refreshed daily, so new stories appear each time you return.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read your digest and chat with Quill">
    At your chosen time, Quorent sends an AI-written email summarizing the most relevant stories from your feed. Open it in any email client — no special app required.

    If you want to go deeper on any topic, open the **Quill** chat in your dashboard. Quill is your AI news correspondent: ask it to summarize a topic, compare stories, or explain something you read. It searches your personal article library to give answers grounded in your actual feed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
