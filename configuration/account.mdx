---
title: "Managing your Quorent account and profile"
description: "View your name and email, save preference changes, sign out of Quorent, or permanently delete your account from the Settings page."
---

The **Account** section in Settings shows the name and email address associated with your Quorent account. This is also where you can save any changes you've made to your preferences, sign out, and — if needed — permanently delete your account.

## Viewing your account information

At the top of the Settings page, the **Account** section displays:

- **Name** — the display name pulled from your sign-in profile
- **Email** — the address Quorent uses to deliver your daily digest

These fields are read-only in Settings. Your name and email come from the Google account you used to sign in and are managed through your Google profile.

## Signing in with Google

Quorent uses Google as its sign-in method. When you first open the app, you authenticate with your Google account. Your session is managed securely, so you stay signed in across visits without needing to log in each time.

## Saving changes

After updating your interests or email digest settings, click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the Settings page. The button label changes to **Saved ✓** briefly to confirm your preferences were stored. If a problem occurs during saving, an error message appears above the button explaining what went wrong.

<Note>
  You must have at least one interest selected for the Save Changes button to be active. If all interests are removed, the button is disabled until you select at least one.
</Note>

## Signing out

To sign out of Quorent, click the **Dashboard** button in the Settings header to return to your Dashboard, then use the sign-out option in the dashboard header. Signing out ends your current session and returns you to the Quorent homepage.

## Deleting your account

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All of your data — including your profile, conversations, messages, and fetched articles — is immediately and irreversibly removed. There is no grace period or recovery option.
</Warning>

The **Danger Zone** section at the bottom of Settings lets you permanently delete your account. Follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scroll to Danger Zone">
    At the bottom of the Settings page, below your other preferences, find the **Danger Zone** section outlined in red.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the warning">
    The section clearly states what will be deleted: your profile, conversations, messages, articles, and all related records. Make sure you understand this action cannot be reversed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type DELETE to confirm">
    In the confirmation field, type the word `DELETE` in capital letters exactly as shown. This step prevents accidental deletions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete Account">
    Click the **Delete Account** button. If the confirmation text does not match, an error prompts you to try again. If it matches, your account deletion begins immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account removed and signed out">
    Once deletion is complete, you are automatically signed out and redirected to the Quorent homepage. Your data has been permanently removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
