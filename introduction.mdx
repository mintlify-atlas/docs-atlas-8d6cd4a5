Quorent is a personalized AI news assistant that turns the firehose of global news into a concise, relevant briefing tailored to your interests. Every day, Quorent pulls articles from thousands of sources, generates an AI-written summary of the most important stories, and sends it to your inbox at the time you choose. When you want to go deeper, Quill — your in-app AI news correspondent — answers questions, surfaces patterns, and helps you make sense of what you're reading.

<Note>
  Quorent is a live, hosted product. You can access it at [quorent-rho.vercel.app](https://quorent-rho.vercel.app) — no installation required.
</Note>

## Key features

**Personalized article feed** — During onboarding you choose up to 10 topics from a list of preset interests or create your own. Quorent fetches fresh, relevant articles every day based on those topics.

**AI email digests** — Each morning, Quorent compiles your articles into a concise AI-generated briefing and delivers it to your inbox at your preferred time and timezone. You get the signal without the noise.

**Quill AI chat** — Quill is your in-app AI correspondent. Ask it to summarize a topic, compare stories, find trends in your feed, or explain a concept — it uses semantic search over your personal article library to give grounded, relevant answers.

**Dashboard** — See your article feed, digest delivery status, and current interests at a glance. Everything is organized around your topics so you can scan quickly and decide what to read.

## How it works

1. You sign in with Google and complete a one-step onboarding.
2. You select your topics and set a daily digest time.
3. Quorent fetches and indexes articles relevant to your interests every day.
4. Your AI digest lands in your inbox at your chosen time.
5. Open the dashboard to browse your feed or start a conversation with Quill.

## Who Quorent is for

Quorent is for people who care about staying informed but don't have time to monitor dozens of sources manually. It works best if you have a clear sense of the topics you follow — the more specific your interests, the more focused your daily digest will be.
