Quorent is a personalized AI news assistant that learns your interests and surfaces the stories that matter most to you. Each day, Quorent fetches articles from thousands of sources, generates an AI-powered digest summary, and delivers it to your inbox. When you want to go deeper, Quill — your in-app AI correspondent — is ready to answer questions, find patterns, and help you understand what you're reading.

## What Quorent does

Quorent connects your interests to the news in three ways:

- **Personalized article feed** — Articles are fetched daily from NewsAPI.ai based on the topics you choose during onboarding. Your feed updates every day with fresh, relevant content.
- **AI email digests** — Every morning, Quorent compiles your articles into a concise, AI-generated summary and sends it to your inbox at your preferred time and timezone.
- **Quill AI chat** — Ask Quill anything about your articles. It uses semantic search to find relevant content and answers your questions in natural language.
